The Princess & The Commoner

A Modern Fairytale in Eleven Chapters

1. The Graceful Princess

Once upon a time, in a world not so far from our own, there lived a princess not the kind who needed rescuing, nor one who sat waiting in a lonely tower. She was strong, graceful, and strikingly beautiful the kind of beauty that wasn’t just skin deep. Her confidence, her poise and the way she carried herself drew eyes wherever she went. People whispered tales about her elegance, her charm and how men from far and wide tried to win her heart. But the princess was no stranger to attention and no stranger to heartbreak either.

In her past, she'd let people into her life with open arms and a kind heart, only to be taken for granted. She wasn’t bitter. Just... wiser now. She wasn’t looking for perfection, but for something real someone who could see her for who she truly was.

1

2. The Ordinary Man

IMiles away in a humble town, a thoughtful commoner lived his simple life. He wasn’t the kind who would catch eyes at first glance. His strength lay in his heart, not his looks. He was awkwardly cute, deeply academic, and someone who felt emotions too intensely.

past breakup had left him bruised, yet hopeful. He wanted something more than just company—he wanted connection. He tried speaking to people on apps, but something always felt off. The laughter never reached his heart. He was still searching for his kindred spirit.

2

3. A Fateful Swipe

One ordinary evening, their lives changed forever. The commoner swiped right on a beautiful profile, not knowing it was the princess in disguise. She matched back instantly.

Their first conversation lasted hours. The princess laughed at his jokes. The commoner was captivated by her intelligence. Neither could explain the immediate connection they felt.

3

4. Midnight Conversations

In an attempt to keep the conversation alive, he sent her a questionnaire—one he had tried with others before. But this time, it mattered. She didn’t respond right away.

Days passed. August 3rd turned to 4th. He checked his phone obsessively, even looked her up on Truecaller, only to find she had premium. He felt silly—but also desperate. And then, when he had nearly given up, a message pinged. She had answered. His heart skipped a beat.

4

5. The Bond Deepens

Their chats deepened. They discovered they shared the same birthday month. They connected on Snapchat, and to his surprise, she stalked his LinkedIn. He blushed like a boy. Their talks spanned topics—politics, cricket, movies, their same school topics.

Their Spotify playlists matched 72%. The commoner deleted the dating app, realizing no one else compared. The princess and commoner found herself smiling at their phone constantly.

5

6. The First Meeting

On September 9th at 6:45 PM, they met at the station. When the commoner saw her, his breath caught - she was even more beautiful in person.

At the cafe, conversation flowed effortlessly. Then came the shocking discovery when he saw her home - not a house, but a palace. His sweet online crush was actual royalty.

6

7. Secret Meetings

They began meeting secretly under an old willow tree. She brought palace cookies. They talked for hours, sharing dreams and childhood memories.

The princess realized this commoner saw her truly - not as a prize to win, but as a person to love. Their goodbye kiss before his departure was magical.

7

8. The Love Letter

On November 4th, before leaving her city again, he wrote her a heartfelt letter and gifted her a stuffed toy they both jokingly called their “kid.” In the letter, he poured his heart out and proposed.

She said yes. She had fallen too deeply, quietly, but truly.

8

9. A Winter Together

From December to February, they were inseparable. They had their first fight and made up stronger.

They celebrated their first holidays together, exchanging simple but meaningful moments. The princess realized she'd never been happier.

9

10. Dreams of Forever

In March, something remarkable happened - the princess, who had never discussed marriage with anyone, found herself planning a future with her commoner.

They imagined a little cottage with a library, travels to distant lands. For the first time, both saw a clear path to happiness - together.

10

11. Happily Ever After

And so the princess who thought she'd never find love, and the commoner who believed he wasn't worthy, created their own fairytale.

Their story continues to grow each day - not perfect, but perfectly theirs. For true love isn't about palaces or titles, but finding the person who makes your heart feel at home.

The End... or rather, The Beginning.
